About

What is TCE?

The Tr채umerei Clarinet Ensemble (TCE) is a student musical group for clarinet players from Middle to High School in New York and New Jersey, and is led by Musical Director Mr. Seung-Ho Choi. We are very proud of the fact that even though students in TCE may not necessarily dream of a professional musical career, their musical skills are comparable to those of any future professional music student. Although most performers pursue musical careers as soloists, TCE focuses on collaboration, respect for each other's music, and skills to pursue the true meaning of a clarinet ensemble.

What does TCE do?

We would like to proudly present this special and unique music concert. TCE is the first ensemble which consists of clarinets only in NY and NJ. We truly believe that you will enjoy the magic of the clarinet with different, but amazing music in TCE concert. It is our hope that the TCE may positively impact the lives of young people who will follow their dreams.
